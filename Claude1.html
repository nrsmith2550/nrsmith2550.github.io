The Method Matters

Pressure moves dirt. Softwashing removes it.

Cranking up the PSI looks satisfying, but on roofs, siding, and painted surfaces it can do more harm than good — and it doesn't touch what's causing the staining in the first place.

Pressure Washing Alone

  • 4,000+ PSI can strip paint, crack shingles, and etch wood
  • Blasts algae off the visible surface — the roots stay put
  • Can force water behind siding, trim, and light fixtures
  • Regrowth is often visible again within 2–3 months

Softwashing (Our Approach)

  • 150 PSI or less — safe for shingles, paint, wood, and stucco
  • Biodegradable solution kills algae, mold & mildew at the root
  • No forced water intrusion behind siding or trim
  • Stays visibly clean 2–4x longer than pressure alone

We still bring the pressure where it belongs — concrete, pavers, and brick can take higher PSI and need it to lift ground-in stains. Softwashing is reserved for the surfaces pressure can damage.

Questions

Frequently asked

What's the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?

Pressure washing uses high PSI water alone — effective on concrete and hard, durable surfaces, but it can damage shingles, paint, and wood. Softwashing uses low pressure plus a cleaning solution that kills algae, mold, and mildew at the root, making it the safer choice for roofs, siding, stucco, and painted surfaces.

Is softwashing safe for my landscaping and pets?

Yes. We use biodegradable, EPA-registered solutions, pre-soak or cover sensitive plants, and recommend pets stay indoors during treatment out of caution. Once an area is rinsed and dry, it's safe for normal use.

How long will the results last?

Softwashed surfaces typically stay visibly clean 2 to 4 times longer than pressure washing alone, since the treatment kills the algae's root system instead of just removing what's visible on the surface.

Do I need to be home for the appointment?

No. As long as we have exterior access and any gates are unlocked, most jobs can be completed without you present. We send before-and-after photos when the work is done.

What areas do you service?

We're based in North Braddock and serve all of Allegheny County, including Pittsburgh, Wilkinsburg, Monroeville, Squirrel Hill, and the surrounding Mon Valley communities.
